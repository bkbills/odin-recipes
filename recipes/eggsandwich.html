Egg Sandwich

Description

The Egg Sandwich is my #1 choice for breakfast. This recipe will show you how I make the Egg Sandwich but keep in mind that a lot of the ingredients are able to be removed or swapped with prefference. This recipe will use youre choice of bread, egge, cheese, tomato, bacon, avocado, canadian bacon or ham.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Egg
  • Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Tomato
  • Canadian bacon or ham
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Prepare a pan for 2 slices of bacon, cook on medium heat.
  2. Get bread, eggs, cheese, tomato, avocado, set on counter.
  3. Cut tomato into slices.
  4. Cut avocado into slices.
  5. Cook egg over easy.
  6. Toast bread.
  7. Put egg on bread.
  8. Put cheese on egg.
  9. Put tomato on cheese.
  10. Put bacon on tomato.
  11. Put avocado on top piece of bread./li>
  12. Put sandwich together and Enjoy!
